Making their run into your favorite local coffeehouse now, these confectionary catches are freshly spawned daily in Pat’s kitchens around the world. We have carefully refined these delightful treats by re-imagining traditional cookie recipes and splicing slight amounts of several, savory species of Seattle salmon into them.

Schools of these salmon-shaped sweets are available now and pair wonderfully with any of your favorite lattes, mochas or americanos. Yes, these two Seattle greats are even greater together. But, if you don’t happen to be a coffee drinker per chance, they will go equally well with any of your drinks of choice. Taking to them like a fish to… well, you get the idea.

And if you really, REALLY want that last bit of Seattle ambiance, well... we’ve got you covered!! Every one of our resident baristas have undergone intensive, one-day training in Seattle, and they will gladly toss your salmon cookies to you from behind the counter, Pike Place Market-like, if you prefer. They almost never miss!!
